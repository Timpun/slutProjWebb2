Porsche 911 GT2RS Weissach

As most know, the Porsche 911 GT2RS is one of the fastest cars around a track in the world but the Weissach edition is even faster. It is a great collectors or drivers car as it both is rare and awesome behind the wheel, it may however not be the most comfortable as it is almost a race car for the streets.

Koenigsegg CCX

Koenigsegg is one of the most revered manifacturers of hypercars. Every car that they have built are great collectors cars since they are extremely rare and will never loose value. The car is made almost entirely of carbon fiber and exotic metals such as titanium which reflects in the price of these cars.

Mitsubishi Lancer Evo VI TM

The Mitsubishi Evo VI Tommi Mäkinen Edition is a rare piece of rally history as the Evo found great success in WRC and so did Tommi Mäkinen who raced for Mitsubishi during parts of his carrer. Mäkinen is one of the most succesful WRC drivers in the world with 24 wins and 45 podiums out of a total of 139 rallies, all that led to 4 world titles in his name.

Honda CBR250RR MC22

This Honda motorcycle may not sound impressive with it's small powerplant with only 250 cubic centimeters engine volume however Honda put some serious engineering in to this thing. The fact of the matter is that it revvs to 20 000 RPM! Due to this high red-line a regular cam chaing would not suffice so the engineers made the dual overhead cams gear driven to keep up with the rest of the engine. Hearing this bike you would not expect it to have so small of an engine.

Kimera Automobili Evo 37

The rally fans have another car here but it may not be what you expect what it is. Although it may look like the iconic Lancia 037 Group B WRC car due to it being designed on the same chassis it is a new car called Evo 37, all components are the best from brands like Öhlins and Brembo with carbon fiber body panels so the car is incredibly low weight at only 1050kg! It is one of only 37 being produced so it is incredibly rare and will rise in value.

Lexus LFA

The incredible Lexus LFA produces one of the best sounding noises know to all of humanity with it's naturally aspirated 4.8L V10. It is a hard feat to even come close to making a car rival the LFA's sound meaning that as more and more cars become electric enthusiasts will be drawn more and more to vehicles like this one.

McLaren P1

The McLaren P1 is one of the cars in "The Holy Trinity" of supercars together with the Porsche 918 Spyder and Ferrari La Ferrari. The P1 has all carbon fiber body panels and monocoque chassis meaning that it is stiff and lightweight, perfect for some spirited track driving. It is according to Supercar Driver when they tested these three cars the fastes of the trinity around a race track when BTCC driver Mat Jackson drove them around the Silverstone circuit.

Cyan P1800

Much like the Kimera Automobili Evo 37 the Cyan P1800 is based on an old car but built to modern sportcar standards with mostly carbon fiber body panels to keep the car down to about 990kg. Cyan is a Swedish touring car race team who has raced Volvos for many years and all that knowledge and experience has been put in to this car in order to make a true drivers car.

Toyota Prius

The infamous Toyota Prius, it is one of the most sold vehicles in the entire world and for good reason. The car has a very efficiet engine and constant variable transmission meaning it can be at the optimal engine speed at all times along with an electric engine meaning it is very good for the environment. This is most likely the best commuters car in the world!

Honda RC213V-S

The Honda RC213V-S is a street legal MotoGP motorcycle, it shares many components with the RC213V that Marc Marquez raced but it has some changes to prolong the service life so you don't need to replace everything after a few hours. With these changes it no longer has a dual clutch gearbox or pneumatic valves and only pushes 159 hp out of the one litre V4 engine or 215 with the race kit installed instead of over 250hp in the MotoGP bike. It weights 170kg without and 160kg with the race kit which you motorcyclists know is very light so this is a perfect bike to rip around a track whilst still being road legal!

Aprilia RSV4 XTrenta

The Aprilia RSV4 is a great superbike for the roads but the Xtrenta takes it to the next level. It upps the power to 230 hp, losses weight to only 166kg, adds wings for downforce and stability and proper race suspension and a SC Project exhaust. If you want to go as fast as possible around a track without an entire MotoGP team then this is the bike for you, but you better strike fast as there is only 100 bikes available.
